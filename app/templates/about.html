What is unsu.com?

unsu.com is a minimalist cryptocurrency wallet designed to make crypto accessible to everyone. No accounts, no downloads, no complex setup. Just a simple web interface that works on any device.

Access your wallet with just a secret phrase (s-phrase) from any web browser. Send and receive cryptocurrency with minimal steps, making it as easy as sending an email.

Why We Built This

The crypto landscape has become unnecessarily complicated. Overwhelming options, exhaustive verification processes, countless chains and exchanges, and ever-present scam risks create barriers to entry.

Our goal is to cut through this noise with a streamlined, user-friendly platform that demystifies crypto transactions. We focus on simplicity and accessibility, making cryptocurrency usable for everyone—not just tech experts.

How It Works

  • Unlock: Enter your secret phrase to access your wallet instantly
  • Send: Enter recipient address (or scan QR code), amount, and send
  • Receive: Share your address or QR code—no extra steps required
  • Bridge: Move USDC between chains - select a destination, enter an amount, and the wallet handles the rest
  • Customize: Add or remove chains and assets, configure RPC endpoints, and personalize your experience
  • ENS Names: Use readable names like "yourname.unsu.eth" instead of long addresses

Bridge USDC

Move USDC between chains directly from the wallet using Circle's Cross-Chain Transfer Protocol (CCTP). No wrapped tokens, no liquidity pools, no slippage. Native USDC is burned on one chain and minted on another.

  • Simple: Select a destination chain, enter an amount, and confirm. The wallet handles approval, burn, attestation, and mint automatically.
  • Resumable: Bridge progress is saved locally. If you close the browser during the 1-5 minute attestation wait, your bridge resumes when you return.
  • All directions: Bridge between any supported CCTP chains - Ethereum, Base, Arbitrum, and Arc.

Arc Network

Arc is a chain where USDC is the native gas token. Instead of needing ETH to pay for transactions, you pay fees in USDC. This removes the friction of acquiring a separate token just for gas, making it simpler for users who primarily transact in stablecoins.

unsu supports Arc Testnet out of the box. Bridge USDC from any supported testnet to Arc and start transacting immediately - no need to find a faucet or swap for a gas token.

Security & Privacy

Your private keys never leave your browser. All wallet operations happen client-side. We don't store your keys, your phrase, or any personal information.

We acknowledge the trade-offs between user-friendliness and security. This wallet is designed for small, everyday transactions. For large amounts, consider a hardware wallet.
